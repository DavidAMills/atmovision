About Us

Our journey and vision.

  • Our Mission

    Healthy Air for a Better Life

    Our mission is to provide real-time high-resolution air quality monitoring and forecasting to empower individuals to avoid harmful air particles and make informed decisions about their environment.

  • Our Solution

    AI-Driven Assistant Tool

    AtmoVision offers a unique solution with real-time and forecast air quality data, empowering you to make informed decisions about your environment. Whether you're planning a run, a commute, or just your daily activities, our AI-driven assistant can guide you to safer choices.

  • Our Architecture

    Seamless Integration

    AtmoVision integrates smoothly into daily routines, sparing customers from the hassle of learning extra hardware and making expensive purchases. Our sophisticated data fusion process generates actionable insights right down to the neighborhood level.

  • Our Business Model

    Freemium Software Model

    Our initial market approach leverages a freemium software model, making our app easily accessible on devices that users already integrate into their daily lives. Once users adopt our application, they can purchase subscription services for premium features like detailed forecasts and historical analysis.
